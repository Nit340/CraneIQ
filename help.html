Getting Started

System Overview

CraneIQ is an advanced monitoring system designed to provide real-time insights into your crane operations. The system tracks multiple parameters to ensure optimal performance and safety.

Key features include:

  • Real-time monitoring of load, vibration, temperature, and energy
  • Historical data analysis with customizable reports
  • Automated alerts for abnormal conditions
  • Predictive maintenance recommendations
  • Multi-user access with role-based permissions

Dashboard Guide

The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your crane's status:

  • Status Cards: Quick view of each crane's current state
  • OEE Metrics: Overall Equipment Effectiveness scores
  • Quick Stats: Power consumption, current draw, and utilization
  • Filter Bar: Quickly sort cranes by status (Working, Idle, Off, etc.)

Click on any crane card to view detailed metrics and historical data.

Operations Log Documentation

Overview

The Operations Log provides a complete record of all crane operations, including hoisting, traveling, and switching activities. This feature helps you track equipment usage, analyze operational patterns, and maintain compliance with safety regulations.

Troubleshooting

Missing operation records

  • Verify the crane's sensors are properly connected
  • Check your date range filters aren't too restrictive
  • Confirm the crane was operational during the selected period

Incorrect operation types

  • Check sensor calibration on the crane
  • Verify the crane configuration in Settings
  • Contact support if issues persist

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up custom alerts for vibration monitoring?

Navigate to the Alerts page from the sidebar. Click "Create Alert" and select "Vibration" as the parameter type. You can set thresholds for:

  • Overall vibration levels (mm/s)
  • Specific frequency ranges (Hz)
  • Individual axes (X, Y, Z)

Choose notification methods (email, SMS, or in-app) and save your alert.

What do the different vibration status colors mean?

The system uses color coding to indicate vibration severity:

  • Green (Normal): Vibration levels are within safe operating limits
  • Yellow (Warning): Vibration approaching dangerous levels - monitor closely
  • Red (Critical): Immediate attention required - potential equipment damage
  • Gray (Offline): Sensor is not reporting data
How often is vibration data updated?

Vibration data is collected and transmitted in real-time:

  • Live monitoring: Updates every 2 seconds
  • Trend charts: 1-minute averages
  • Historical data: Stored at 5-minute intervals
  • Spectrum analysis: Updated every 30 seconds
Can I export vibration data for analysis?

Yes! You can export vibration data in multiple formats:

  • CSV: Raw time-series data for all axes
  • Excel: Formatted reports with charts
  • PDF: Professional reports for documentation
  • API: Direct integration with other systems (Pro plan)

Go to the Reports page to generate and download your data.

How far back does the operations log store data?

Data retention depends on your plan:

  • Basic: 30 days of operational data
  • Advanced: 6 months of operational data
  • Pro: Unlimited data retention
Can I track multiple cranes simultaneously?

Yes! The system supports monitoring of multiple cranes:

  • Each crane is identified by its unique ID
  • Filters allow you to view individual cranes or all at once
  • Reports can be generated for specific cranes or groups
How accurate are the operation duration measurements?

Duration measurements are highly accurate:

  • Recorded to the nearest second
  • Based on sensor activation timestamps
  • Automatically adjusted for any system latency
